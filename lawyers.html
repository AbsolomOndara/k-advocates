Who We Are

Keniz Agira & Company Advocates is a leading full-service law firm in Kenya, recognized for delivering exceptional legal services tailored to diverse clients. Our clientele includes individuals, dynamic corporations, esteemed financial institutions, governmental, and non-governmental organizations, each with unique legal needs. Whether you are a local business or an international investor, our expert legal team is well-equipped to navigate the complexities of Kenyan law and cross-border legal matters. As a top choice for international legal services, we specialize in providing comprehensive guidance that meets the specific requirements of our varied client base.

Our Team

Our partners bring a wealth of experience and diverse educational backgrounds, contributing vibrant perspectives to our practice. This diversity enables us to respond effectively to the evolving demands of a globalized economy. With an experienced legal team, we offer innovative solutions designed for both local and international clients, covering multiple sectors, including corporate law, financial regulations, and investment law. We are your trusted partner for navigating the Kenyan legal landscape.

Location

Strategically located in Kenya, a pivotal business hub in East Africa, we expertly navigate the complex legal, economic, and socio-political environments. Kenya's reputation as a gateway to Africa makes it an attractive destination for foreign investors and multinational corporations. At Keniz Agira & Company Law Advocates LLP, we leverage these advantages to provide practical and innovative solutions tailored to meet your distinct legal needs, whether you're seeking assistance with foreign investments or navigating Kenyan business laws.

Commitment to Excellence

We prioritize delivering pragmatic solutions grounded in a deep understanding of our clients' goals and challenges. Our client-centric approach ensures every legal strategy aligns with your objectives, whether you're a startup, multinational corporation, or government entity. We are committed to building long-term relationships based on trust, efficiency, and success. Explore our success stories to see how we empower businesses to thrive in Kenya and beyond.
