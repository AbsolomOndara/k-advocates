Who We Are

Kagira Advocates is a premier full-service law firm in Kenya, recognized for delivering exceptional legal services tailored to diverse clients. Our clientele includes individuals, dynamic corporations, esteemed financial institutions, governmental, and non-governmental organizations, each with unique legal needs. Whether you are a local business or an international investor, our expert legal team is well-equipped to navigate the complexities of Kenyan law and cross-border legal matters. As a top choice for international legal services, we specialize in providing comprehensive guidance that meets the specific requirements of our varied client base.

Our Team

Our partners bring a wealth of experience and diverse educational backgrounds, contributing vibrant perspectives to our practice. This diversity enables us to respond effectively to the evolving demands of a globalized economy. With an experienced legal team, we offer innovative solutions designed for both local and international clients, covering multiple sectors, including corporate law, financial regulations, and investment law. We are your trusted partner for navigating the Kenyan legal landscape.
