Our Journey Through Time

Two decades of growth, excellence, and unwavering commitment to legal practice

2003

Foundation & Establishment

Kagira Advocates was founded with a vision to provide comprehensive legal services. Started with a small team of dedicated lawyers focusing on corporate and commercial law, quickly establishing a reputation for excellence.

2008

Expansion & Growth

Expanded our practice areas to include litigation, real estate, and intellectual property. Opened our second office location and grew our team to 15 legal professionals, serving an increasingly diverse client base.

2015

Regional Recognition

Recognized as one of Kenya's leading full-service law firms. Received multiple awards for legal excellence and expanded our international practice, serving clients across East Africa and beyond.

2020

Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital solutions to enhance client service delivery. Launched virtual consultation services and advanced case management systems, ensuring seamless service during global challenges.

2024

Future Forward

Continuing our legacy of excellence with expanded practice areas in technology law, environmental compliance, and international arbitration. Committed to innovation and maintaining our position as legal industry leaders.
